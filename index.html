Why do most diet plans fail?

Most diet plans don't work. For 3 simple reasons:

  • They're based on myths and rumours about how your body works. In other words, they're simply wrong about what is and isn't healthy.
  • The diet plan generally works, but it isn't the best one for you.
  • You aren't putting in the work required for the plan to show results.

There's no easy way out of this. If you want results, you have to do the work. Improving your health involves building good habits and breaking bad ones. It's hard, but not as hard as you might think.

You don't have to starve. You don't have to eat anything you hate. You don't have to sacrifice every sweet and chocolate and burger to be healthy. And you certainly don't have to rely on pills with nasty side-effects. You can reduce your risk of diseases, live a more energetic and productive life, and be happy with your diet. It's your choice.
